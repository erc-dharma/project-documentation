# About this platform

This platform was the principal deliverable of an ERC Synergy Project called DHARMA. Since the end of that project, the platform is being managed by a consortium which retains the same name.

# About the ERC Synergy Project DHARMA

The DHARMA project [ERC n°809994](https://doi.org/10.3030/809994) was
launched on May 1st 2019. It lasted over 90 months project (May 2019 through Octiber 2026) and was funded by an ERC
Synergy Grant 2018 through the European Union’s Research and Innovation Programme Horizon 2020. For general information about the project and its goals,
head over to [our blog on Hypotheses.org](https://dharma.hypotheses.org). The acronym DHARMA stood for The Domestication of “Hindu” Asceticism and the Religious Making of South and Southeast Asia.
